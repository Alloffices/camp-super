Our Mission

Looking for the BEST camp for your kids this summer?

At Camp Super Duper, your child’s development and entertainment is our main priority. Childhood is a special time in kids’ lives; a time to grow through exciting activities and learn from educational experiences. Camp Super Duper is dedicated to helping your child enjoy this wonderful age as well as helping them develop cognitively, emotionally and physically!

Click Here to Sign Up!

Campers are encouraged to:


  • BE A KID

    At Camp Super Duper, we understand that you are only a kid once. Our campers are encouraged to embrace the care-free and fun nature of childhood! We provide opportunities for children to learn and develop while enjoying fun activities.

  • EXPRESS THEIR CREATIVITY

    One of our focuses at Camp Super Duper is to ensure that children learn to look within for inspiration. Each child has a unique perspective and we want to teach them to find it and embrace their creativity!

  • BE THEMSELVES

    After teaching our campers to look within to find a special and unique individual, we encourage them to be compassionate towards others and show them ways to communicate effectively. This way they start to understand that it is best to be YOURSELF!
