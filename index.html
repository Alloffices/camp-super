What is camp super duper all about?

Looking for the BEST Camp for Your Kids? Our Summer Sessions Run June 17th through Aug. 16th!

Camp Super Duper is a 21st century camp for 21st century kids! The future is about innovation, creativity, collaboration, emotional intelligence and grit – and we get that. Our one-of-a-kind program is designed to nurture curiosity, foster connection, inspire and feed creativity, build self esteem – and have nonstop, milk-out-the-nose crazy fun! Our camp’s safe, loving, zany environment and amazing adult camp counselors keep parents and kids coming back year after year. Join us this summer!
